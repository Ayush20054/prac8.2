Monster Truck

A monster truck is a specialized off-road vehicle with a heavy-duty suspension, four-wheel steering, large-displacement V8 engines, and oversized tires constructed for competition and entertainment uses.

Monster trucks are built for power, so it's no surprise they can get some good air when navigating a well-built track. They can jump a few dozen feet vertically in the air, and some of the longest distance jumps recorded have been upward of 200 feet. Did you enjoy these monster truck facts?
